Welcome to the Product Knowledge Base

The PKB is a single place for everyone and anyone looking to find game and product information without jumping between Confluence, Contentful, and a dozen other tabs. It pulls live data from multiple sources so what you see is always up to date.

Finding your way around
Product
Everything in the Product section comes live from Confluence. Use it to browse verticals, find process docs, and anything else the product team maintains. Content is always in sync. If it's been updated in Confluence, it's updated here.
Tools
Purpose-built tools for day to day use.
The Bingo Schedule gives you a full 8 day view of UK and Spain streams with ticket prices, prize structures, and special features.
Game Sheets lets you search and filter the full live game catalogue from Contentful, find games by venture, provider, game type, platform, and more, then pull up a full game sheet with all available metadata from active games for UK and Spain.
Links
Quick access to external systems like Confluence and Contentful, one click away.
Feedback
Use the Share Feedback button in the top right corner at any time. It's anonymous and open to anything: corrections, missing content, suggestions, whatever's on your mind. Everything submitted is reviewed and helps shape what gets built next.
Search
The search bar at the top searches across both Confluence and Contentful at the same time. Results are returned as cards and mixed by default. Use the filter buttons at the top of the results to narrow down by source: All, Confluence, or Contentful. Clicking a result opens it directly in the app, with a link available if you want to view it in the original source. This is a single page app so there is no browser back button. To return to your results, press Enter in the search bar again.
A few things to know
RTP data is incomplete across most games. This is a known data quality issue in Contentful. The data exists but has not been consistently populated. We are working on finding the right source to connect it properly.
Game features shown on game sheets reflect what the game is capable of, not necessarily what is active on site. Some features may be disabled per market, for example Bonus Buy.
What's coming
An AI assistant is in development that will let you chat directly with the knowledge base. Ask questions and get answers pulled from across Confluence, Contentful, and more, without having to search manually.
Game Sheets will get additional filters including Win Line Type and Features, making it easier to find games by mechanic.
RTP data will be connected once the right source is identified and accessible.
