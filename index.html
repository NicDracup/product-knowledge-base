Product Knowledge Base

A central reference for Bally's iGaming product team covering our full game portfolio across all verticals.

Confluence page — always up to date

This space brings together plain English guides on our games, product types, and mechanics — written for anyone in the business, whether you're a new starter trying to understand the difference between a Megaways slot and a crash game, or an experienced team member who needs a quick reference on how Jackpot Blast works.

What's covered

  • Slots, Live Casino, Arcade, Jackpots, Bingo, and Multiplayer verticals
  • Per-game overviews, key mechanics, and venture availability
  • Product type explainers in plain English

How it works

Content is owned and maintained by vertical leads across the product team. Game data is pulled from Contentful and documentation from Confluence. Anyone can browse by vertical or ask the AI assistant a question and get an instant answer.
